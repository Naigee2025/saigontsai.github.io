About Me
Sr. Director at Google Hardware with over 25 years of R&D expertise. I specialize in overcoming complex challenges at the intersection of ID and RF engineering, including advanced antenna systems, interference mitigation, and EMI solutions. My career spans the entire evolution of mobile connectivity—participating in every major generational shift from 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G to cutting-edge mmWave technologies.

At Google, I led the wireless hardware integration for every generation of the Pixel phone (Pixel 1 to present). Currently, I oversee the wireless engineering strategy for the entire Google Hardware portfolio (Pixel, Fitbit, Nest, Home), managing a comprehensive technology stack that includes Cellular, GNSS, WiFi, Bluetooth, UWB, and NFC.

關於我 (About Me)
擁有超過 25 年無線通訊硬體研發資歷的 Google 硬體資深總監。 我專精於解決極具挑戰性的工業設計 (ID) 與天線整合難題，包含複雜的訊號干擾與電磁相容 (EMI) 處理。我的職涯親身參與了行動通訊技術的每一個重大轉折點——從 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G 一路到最新的毫米波 (mmWave) 技術。

在 Google，我主導了從第一代 Pixel 至今每一代手機的無線硬體整合。目前統籌 Google 全系列硬體產品 (Pixel, Fitbit, Nest, Home) 的無線工程設計與策略，管轄範疇涵蓋 Cellular, GNSS, WiFi, Bluetooth (BT), Ultra-Wideband (UWB) 以及 NFC 等全方位無線技術。
